Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2023

At Web IDE, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us for support. This may include your email address, name, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Service.
  • To notify you about changes to our Service.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To monitor the usage of the Service.

3. Local Data Storage

Web IDE is designed to run locally on your device. Your code projects, files, and assets are stored on your device's local storage and are not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to export or sync them.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@webide.app.
